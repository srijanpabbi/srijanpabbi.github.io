Centre for Electronics Design and Technology

Center for Electronics Design and Technology

CEDT is a "maker space/open access lab" run by Prof. Dhananjay Gadre at Netaji Subhas Institute of technology (NSIT) New Delhi. Here we work towards making our own projects to get a hands on experience for every step from start to finish. We also make pedagogy material for others to work on and learn, and hold regular workshops at our college for spreading knowledge. The longest continuing program we hold thrice a year is our TI-CEPD summer/winter program, where we try to give a kick start into the world of embedded systems.
